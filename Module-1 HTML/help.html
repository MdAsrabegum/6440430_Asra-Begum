How to Use This Portal

  • Register for Events: Go to the "Contact" section, fill out your name, email, and select an event type.
  • See Past Events: Visit the "Events" section for photos and memories from previous community gatherings.
  • Contact Us: Use the provided phone, Instagram, Facebook, or WhatsApp links in the "Contact" section.
  • Give Feedback: Scroll to the "Feedback" section to share your thoughts and suggestions about the event.
  • Watch Videos: See promo videos in the "Event Promo Video" section to get a glimpse of what's coming.

Still have questions? Email us at communityevents@example.com
